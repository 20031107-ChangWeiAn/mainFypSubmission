How do I view my wallet balance or points I have accumulated so far? [BUYER]

You can view your wallet balance or points under the profile page simply by clicking the top left button and it will lead you to your account profile page.

How do I manage return/refund requests from buyers? [SELLER]

Upon receiving a return/refund request from a buyer, you can choose to:

  • Accept the request
  • Counter-propose/negotiate the buyer’s offer
  • Dispute the request

    • You will have to respond to the request within 2 days. If you failed to do so, the buyer’s request will be automatically approved and the refund will be processed accordingly. Depending on the nature of the return/refund request, the buyer may be required to return the products before the refund can be processed.
    For incomplete orders, buyers can opt for partial refund (subject to WorldBay’s review) or return the product(s) for a full refund. For damaged or wrong orders, buyers will be asked to return the product(s) by default in exchange for a full refund. If you do not require your product(s) to be returned, you can discuss and negotiate with the buyer on other arrangements, such as partial refunds without return. If the buyer accepts your proposal, they will receive your proposed refund amount and not need to return the product(s). If they reject your proposal, they may return the product(s) either to you or the WorldBay Warehouse. Either you or the warehouse team will perform a quality check to assess if the products meet the return criteria, before they can be approved for refund. For non-receipt orders, if you wish to dispute the request, you may prepare supporting documents indicating proof of delivery, if applicable.

    When can I report a rating given to me? [SELLER]

    WorldBay believes that proper ratings are beneficial to our entire community. However, you may report ratings to our customer service team if your rating contains the following:

  • Rating contains inappropriate language (e.g. profanities, hate speech)
  • Rating contains inappropriate picture & Video (e.g. Blank or irrelevant picture or video)
  • Rating contains spam text (e.g. Excessive usage of punctuation marks and emoji)
  • Rating contains private information (e.g. addresses, phone numbers, bank accounts)
    • Please allow 5 working days for the Shopee Team to review and reply. While we understand that negative ratings (especially a 1-star rating without comments) can be aggravating to sellers trying to achieve a high shop rating, Shopee will not take action on ratings unless they fall under the above scenarios. WorldBay will proceed to hide the reviews that contains the following:
  • Rating contains inappropriate language (e.g. profanities, hate speech)
  • Rating contains inappropriate picture & Video (e.g. Blank or irrelevant picture or video)
  • Rating contains spam text (e.g. Excessive usage of punctuation marks and emoji)
    • *Deletion of ratings for the above violations will need consent from buyer/seller.
    **WorldBay will proceed to delete for Rating contains private information without any consent.

    We strongly encourage sellers to contact buyers directly to better understand the reason for the negative rating. Buyers may choose to change their rating after your follow-up actions.
